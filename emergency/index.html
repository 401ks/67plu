Emergency Plumbing FAQs

What qualifies as an emergency?

Burst pipes, active flooding, sewage backup, complete water loss, or gas/water heater leaks. If in doubt, WhatsApp us – we'll advise instantly.

Is the callout fee charged if you can't fix it?

Yes. The callout/dispatch fee covers travel, diagnostics, and on-site assessment. It applies regardless of outcome. Full work quote provided before repair begins.

Do you work nights/weekends?

Yes. 24/7 dispatch. Same hourly rates, same guaranteed response time.

What if I need parts?

Techs carry common emergency parts. If specialized parts are needed, we secure temporary fix or source within 24 hrs.
